You may optionally include Auto-Refill to simplify the renewal process and save 10% on your plan.

    Service Protection

    Avoid accidentally losing your service plan and phone number by enrolling in our Service Protection Plan. If you enroll today, you will automatically receive a 30-day extension on your service if you forget to add an airtime card before the end of your service period. Enrollment is free. You pay the $5.99 fee only if you reach your Service End Date.

    A Simple Plan for Every Month

    This is something you're already used to but with no contracts or activation and cancellation fees. If you have a more consistent phone-usage schedule or if you want to share minutes with your family, a Monthly Value plan is perfect for you. You can use the airtime monthly and if you happen to not use up your minutes by the end of your billing period, you can take advantage of Unlimited Carryover®, included in every Montly Value plan, and add them to your next month. Choosing a family plan will give you a 40% discount on airtime for all additional phones.
